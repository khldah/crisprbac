CRISPRBac

A machine learning-powered CRISPR gRNA design tool for bacterial genomes

*Sequence in FASTA format
*The choosing of PAM depends on the Cas9 used in your research. For example, SpCas9 recognize only NGG sequence.
*If there is no match, please select the most closest genome with yours. This is important for genome-wide off-target analysis.
